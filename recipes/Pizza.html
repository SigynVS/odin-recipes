Odin Recipes

Prep: 15 min
Cook: 15 min
Servings: 4

Description

This classic homemade pizza is loaded with fresh ingredients and bursting with flavor. The crust is crispy on the outside while remaining soft and chewy inside. Topped with a rich tomato sauce, melted mozzarella, and your favorite toppings, this pizza is sure to be a hit at any meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1 tsp dried oregano

Instructions

Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).

Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.

Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.

Sprinkle shredded mozzarella and arrange toppings.

Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.

Slice and enjoy your homemade pizza!
